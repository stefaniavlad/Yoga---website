OUR CLASSES

POWER YOGA

You will experience innovative combinations of asanas and meditation techniques, all in a fluid and harmonious rhythm. You will discover a variety of movement sequences that will help your body become more flexible and stronger.

MEDITATION

Meditation focuses on cultivating awareness and inner peace through various techniques, including mindfulness, visualization, and mantra repetition.

GENTLE YOGA FLOW

With slow and graceful movements, accessible postures and appropriate breathing and meditation techniques, this class will guide you on a profound journey towards relaxation and stress relief.

YIN YOGA

In yin yoga you are with yourself. You can set your intention, you can ask the question that is bothering you and then while you spend the next 50 minutes, you with your body in various positions, listen to the messages it sends you.

KUNDALINI YOGA

Kundalini Yoga is not just a simple practice, but a deep journey into your inner universe that will connect you with your life force. This unique form of yoga focuses on the energy in the body, using powerful breathing exercises, physical postures, fluid movements.

ZEN REFORMER

Pilates on the Reformer is specially designed to help you release tension built up throughout the day, combining slow and controlled movements with deep breathing techniques. You will experience a series of exercises designed to improve your flexibility and balance your posture.
